Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026
The short version: Aura doesn't collect your personal data, doesn't have a server, and doesn't sell or share anything about you. Everything stays on your device. This policy explains that in full.

What Aura is

Aura is a personal audio player that plays YouTube videos as audio only. It runs entirely in your browser as a single HTML file. There is no backend server, no database, and no account system connected to the internet.

Data we collect

We collect nothing. Aura does not transmit any personal information to us. Your account details, playlists, library, and preferences are stored locally on your device using localStorage — they never leave your browser.

  • Accounts — stored locally on your device only
  • Playlists & Library — stored locally on your device only
  • Preferences & settings — stored locally on your device only
  • Passwords — stored as a local hash, never transmitted
Because all data lives on your device, if you clear your browser's storage or use a different device, your data will not be there. There is no way for us to recover it — we never had it.

Third-party services

Aura uses two Google services to function:

  • YouTube IFrame API — used to play audio from YouTube. When you play a video, your request goes to YouTube's servers. YouTube's own privacy policy applies to that interaction. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • YouTube Data API v3 — used for the in-app search feature. When you search, your query is sent to Google's API. No personal account data is included. See Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not use analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, cookies, or any other third-party data tools.

Cookies

Aura does not use cookies. We use localStorage — a browser feature that stores data only on your device — to save your settings and content between sessions. This is not a tracking mechanism.

Children's privacy

Aura does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, there is nothing to protect beyond what YouTube's own policies cover when content is played.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Since we don't collect contact information, we can't notify you directly — but the latest version will always be at aura.miguelito4u.com/privacy.html.
